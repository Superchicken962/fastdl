Guidelines

Please read all of the guidelines and follow them while sending your file(s) or else they wont be uploaded.


1. Be Patient

Your files will be uploaded as soon as possible. Don't rush it.

2. Respect Everyone

Pretty simple, just respect everyone and have an overall good atitude! (I mean, you are getting this for free!)

3. Format email nicely

Not as big of a guideline as others but it is appreciated if you make your message tidy and easy to read.

4. No explicit, defamatory or racist content

We will not accept any explicit content (such as gore, pornography, racial slurs)

5. Accept file declines

If your file gets declined and doesn't get uploaded, don't throw a tantrum. You can ask why it was declined, but still do this in a nice manner.

6. No demanding

Demanding doesn't help in anyway and can get you temporarily banned from requesting files.

7. Try to compress your files (Courtesy)

This is not necessary at all, however it would be greatly appreciated if you did compress your files into a .bz2 format before sending them.


Thank you for reading the guidelines, enjoy our fastDL server!
